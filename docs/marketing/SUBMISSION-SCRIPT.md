# Rally — UXmaxx submission film, VO script + shot map (~110s)

Draft v1 for founder edit. Faceless VO over the running product (the format UXmaxx judges reward). Product-only, no human b-roll. Follows the winning-pitch skeleton: what-it-is → problem → the magic UX → features-tied-to-sponsor-tech → the trust beat → differentiation close. Written to be SPECIFIC to Rally, not generic. ~300 words ≈ 110s at a calm read.

VO tone: calm certainty — not hype. The quiet confidence of a mechanism that can't hold your money hostage. Pauses are deliberate. Sound: low warm hum under the "held/pooled" beats, a bright transient on every payout/refund.

## Notes for the edit
- **Every claim is true + shown.** The bar rise (beat 3) and the refund (beat 6) are REAL on-chain state (circle #2 was broken on purpose; the chip-in via the automated-e2e OTP flow) — this is the "no placeholders / it's real" that scores the completeness + UX weight.
- **Sponsor tech named as the REASON it feels smooth, not as a feature list** (beat 4) — exactly what the UXmaxx thesis "no seed phrases, just maxx UX" rewards.
- **Script is human-owned:** founder edits every line for his voice before it's recorded. This draft is the specific, product-grounded starting point (the research said the failure mode is a generic AI script — this is deliberately Rally-specific).
- **The one sentence the whole film serves:** "pays out together, or refunds everyone — automatically."
- Runtime target ~105s; trims to ~90s by tightening beats 2 + 5 if needed. Comfortably under the 5-min UXmaxx cap; front-loaded for judges watching many back-to-back.
